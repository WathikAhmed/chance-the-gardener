Chance the Gardener

This application was developed as part of an undergraduate capstone project by group PS2102 of Western Sydeny University. The capstone project aims to challenge students by requiring us to utilise all our knowledge and skills gained throughout our studies.

We are honoured to have gotten the chance to work with Jonathon Wolfe to bring his innovative idea to life.
